# Khandera Manifesto

We are not a gallery. We are a catalyst.

We build temporary autonomous stages from dust, code, protest memories, and unresolved city noise.

## Why we exist

- To disrupt passive spectatorship.
- To refuse extractive art systems.
- To create collective authorship and shared ritual.

## How we work

We run interventions across abandoned rooms, rooftops, and improvised neighborhood nodes. We combine projection, audio, text fragments, paint, and computational systems to produce open-ended experiences.

## Invitation

If your practice seeks friction, tenderness, and structural disobedience, collaborate with us.
